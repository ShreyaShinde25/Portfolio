About Me.

Hello! I'm Shreya Shinde, a passionate and results-driven Computer Engineering graduate from the University of Waterloo. My journey into the world of technology began during my bachelor's in Electrical Engineering, where I discovered my true calling lay in coding and building innovative solutions.

In my final year, I stumbled upon a computer-oriented project, "Data-Driven Modeling of Lithium-Ion Batteries." Taking the lead, I delved into coding, despite initially lacking knowledge in machine learning. Seeking guidance from computer science professors, online courses, and sheer determination, I developed an LSTM model, structured data in Matlab, and tested it against real-time battery data. This experience ignited my love for coding, prompting me to pursue a Master's in Computer Engineering at the University of Waterloo immediately after my bachelor's.

In the last 16 months of my master's journey, I've not only conquered academic challenges but also delved into real-world projects, learning new technologies, and transforming theoretical knowledge into tangible solutions. My GitHub profile is a testament to this, adorned with green dots showcasing my dedication to continuous learning and innovation.

Currently, I proudly serve as a Research Associate in the e Software Technologies Applied Research (STAR) Lab, collaborating with brilliant minds to push the boundaries of software development. From someone who once had no Git knowledge to now thriving in a research environment, my journey is a testament to the transformative power of relentless curiosity and a love for coding.
